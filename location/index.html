The Old Chapel, Pierce Lane, Fulbourn, Cambridge. CB1 5DJ

Fulbourn is a tranquil village situated just 5 miles from the historic Cambridge city centre. It has a good selection of traditional pubs with restaurants, an excellent butcher selling local produce, a greengrocer with fruit and vegetables from his own farm and a variety of other shops including a Co-op, Post Office and cash point. The surrounding area includes excellent walks particularly through the local nature reserve.

The bus service to central Cambridge via Addenbrooke’s hospital and Cambridge station runs every 20 minutes from the bus stop just opposite.

Cambridge train station (10 minutes by car or 20 minutes by bike) offers services to London every 30 minutes with journey times of less than 50 minutes. The village is also well served by several taxi firms.

London Stansted Airport is 25 minutes drive away.
