The Old Chapel, Fulbourn

Self catering accommodation near Cambridge, UK

Accommodation

The Old Chapel comprises:

Downstairs

  • Sitting room with two sofas, TV, DVD player, iPod dock and radio
  • Kitchen with gas cooker, microwave, fridge/freezer
  • Dining area with round table and 4 chairs

Master Bedroom

Upstairs

  • Master bedroom with double bed, chest of drawers, large built-in cupboard with hanging space and shelves, iron, ironing board, hair drier
  • Second bedroom with single bed, wardrobe and desk
  • Bathroom with bath (mixer and shower attachment)

General

The Old Chapel also has:
  • Gas central heating throughout
  • Internet and WiFi
  • Two bikes with locks and helmets
  • Barbecue, outside table and chairs
  • Shared use of the 1/2 acre garden and wood.
